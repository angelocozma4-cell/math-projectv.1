Factoring Techniques

Learn different types of factoring and when to apply each method. Below you'll find explanations and sample problems.

Greatest Common Factor
Find the largest factor shared by all terms.
Example: 6x + 9 ⇒ 3(2x + 3)
Factoring Trinomials
Look for pairs of numbers that multiply to the last term and add to the middle term.
Example: x² + 5x + 6 ⇒ (x + 2)(x + 3)
Difference of Squares
Any expression of the form a² - b² factors to (a + b)(a - b).
Example: x² - 9 ⇒ (x + 3)(x - 3)
