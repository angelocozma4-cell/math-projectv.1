Polynomials & Exponents

Master the rules of polynomials and exponent operations. Below you'll find explanations, key concepts, and sample problems.

What is a Polynomial?
A polynomial is an expression made of variables, coefficients, and exponents, combined using addition, subtraction, and multiplication. Example: 2x³ + 4x - 7
Exponent Rules
  • Product Rule: am · an = am+n
  • Power Rule: (am)n = amn
  • Quotient Rule: am / an = am-n
Sample Problem
Simplify: x² · x³
Solution: x² · x³ = x2+3 = x5
